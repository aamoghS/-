My Experiences

  • laptop NLP Logix(2020)

My most recent internship was with NLP Logix in the Summer of 2023 as part of the NLP Logix Summer Intership. This intership was in-person at Jacksonville, Flordia

During my internship at NLP Logix, I leveraged Tableau to produce sophisticated data visualizations by analyzing the NFL Play-by-Play dataset from 2001-2021. I identified trends and established relationships within the data by filtering over 30,000 columns, building interactive dashboards, and designing relation patterns. This allowed for a comprehensive understanding of the data and facilitated more informed decision-making processes.

In addition to data visualization, I developed a machine learning algorithm in Python, utilizing Random Forest Classifier learning models. I implemented Train-Test splits and parameter tuning to create a predictive model for play type based on various analytic parameters. This model aimed to enhance the strategic planning of the Jacksonville Jaguars by predicting play calls in future games.

My internship culminated in a presentation to the executive analytics team of NLP Logix and the Jacksonville Jaguars. During this presentation, I detailed the model's development process, discussed the significance of feature importance, and explained the confusion matrices to demonstrate the model's accuracy and reliability in recognizing and predicting play calls. This experience provided valuable insights into the practical applications of data science and machine learning in the sports industry.
